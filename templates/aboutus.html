Hello! We’re Leafnow.

Our Mission: Farming has been in our generations, but the city life has given a more trendy approach to it,GARDENING as we all know it. Keeping in mind the changing times “Leafnow” provides a one stop shop to your gardening needs whilst being rooted.

Our Vision: Leafnow is an initiative to encourage gardening to build a greener society, something our next generation shouldn’t be deprived off.We aim to bring in a platform that can connect plant enthusiasts and help them exchange, buy, sell and donate plants or seeds as part of the green initiative.

A Greek Proverb goes like
"A society grows great when old men plant trees whose shade they know they shall never sit in."
But we are still young, so, if you have the means to grow plants / trees, start digging we say.
